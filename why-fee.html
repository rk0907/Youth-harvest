Why Do We Ask About the 2% Fee?

Transparency, trust, and sustainable operations — Here's the honest breakdown.

What is the 2% Fee?

Paystack Processing

1.95% + GH₵ 0.10

Payment gateway fee for secure card/mobile money processing

Fraud Protection

Included in above

Bank-level encryption, fraud detection, and security monitoring

PCI Compliance

Included in above

Industry-standard payment security certification

Refund Support

Included in above

24/7 dispute resolution and refund processing

Why We're Transparent About This

1

Respect Your Generosity

You deserve to know exactly where your money goes. When you give GH₵ 100, we want 100% reaching our mission — not hidden fees reducing impact.

2

Sustainable Operations

Payment processing costs are real. By asking supporters to cover them, we avoid redirecting ministry funds to administrative overhead.

3

Industry Standard

Leading nonprofits and churches (World Vision, Compassion Int'l, American Red Cross) all disclose processing fees transparently.

4

Your Choice

It's completely optional. You can donate without covering the fee, and we'll gratefully accept every contribution regardless.
