Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation secure?

Yes. We use Paystack, Ghana's leading payment gateway, with bank-level encryption and fraud protection.

Can I donate anonymously?

While we require contact information for payment, your donation can be marked as private in our records.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, Airtel), and bank transfers via Paystack.

Will I receive a receipt?

Yes. An email receipt will be sent immediately after your successful donation for your records.

Can I cancel a recurring donation?

Absolutely. You can manage or cancel recurring donations anytime from your email confirmation link.

Is the transaction fee mandatory?

No. You can choose not to cover the fee, but we encourage it so 100% reaches our mission.
